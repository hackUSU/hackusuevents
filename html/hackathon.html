COMING THIS NOVEMBER

HackUSU is developing an environment for technology innovation.This is where students and employers come together at Utah State University to create the future of tech in Utah.

FAQ

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event for students at all skill levels; which encourages learning new technology, collaborating with other students, and creating something mind-blowing.

How can I register?

Pre-registration will open in JULY. Check back to our home page to find the link when it becomes available.

Who is invited?

All undergraduate students from any university are welcome. Non-USU students must be 18 or older.

How much is it?

The event is FREE TO ALL UNIVERSITY STUDENTS and includes food, drinks, and a bunch of swag for the entire hackathon!

What should I bring?

All attendees are required to bring their valid student ID for admission (name must match registration) and a laptop for hacking.

Are there any rules?

USU is a dry campus (no alcohol) but we'll have lots of energy drinks! Please no firearms, knives, or weapons of any kind. Also, we expect all hackers to follow MLH's Code of Conduct.

What food will you provide?

All meals from lunch the opening day to dinner on the last day, as well as snacks 24/7, are covered by us. We'll try to accommodate dietary restrictions as best we can!

Other Questions?

You can get in touch with us at hackusu@gmail.com or via Twitter, or Facebook.
